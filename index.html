Plug in your ESP32 Cheap Yellow Display, click one button, done. No drivers, no IDE, no command line.

Step 01

Flash the firmware

Connect the board via USB, click install, select the serial port, wait 30 seconds.

MakerStats CYD

Ready
  1. Plug in via Micro-USB data cable
  2. Click Connect & Install
  3. Select port — USB Serial or CH340
  4. Wait ~30s — device reboots when done
Web Serial requires Chrome or Edge on desktop.
Serial access blocked. Check browser settings.
Step 02

Connect to WiFi

  1. Device creates a "MakerStats" WiFi hotspot
  2. Connect your phone or laptop to it
  3. Portal opens — if not, go to 192.168.4.1
  4. Pick your home WiFi, enter password, tap Save
Note  Hotspot closes after ~3 min. Press RST to retry. ESP32 is 2.4 GHz only.
Step 03

Add your username

  1. Tap the gear icon on the dashboard
  2. Tap + Add User
  3. Type your MakerWorld username (no @ needed)
  4. Tap checkmark — stats appear in seconds

Troubleshooting

Common issues

No port appears
Try a different USB cable — many are charge-only. Mac users may need the CH340 driver.
Stuck at "Preparing"
Hold the BOOT button on the board while clicking Install.
No WiFi hotspot
Press RST to reboot. Hotspot appears on first boot or after WiFi reset in Settings.
Won't connect to WiFi
ESP32 supports 2.4 GHz only. Check your router isn't 5 GHz exclusive.
"User not found"
Check spelling. Your MakerWorld profile must be public.
"Cloudflare challenge"
Temporary server block. Device retries automatically with exponential backoff.
